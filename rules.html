Server Rules

  1. Respect all admins
  2. Any form of hacking/cheating is a permanently bannable offence.
  3. Any form of scripting/macroing will result in a 2 week ban for first offences. Followed by a permanent ban on repeat offences.
  4. Any form of toxicity/hate speech/racism/discrimination will not be tolerated.
  5. No chat/mic spaming.
  6. Do not use unpronounceable names or names consisting of invisible or foreign characters.
  7. No Advertisement of any form.
  8. No exploitation or abuse of any plugins, maps, server/game features.
  9. No AFK farming of any kind
  10. No ghosting or stream sniping.
  11. Any form of false accusations are not allowed (this includes false reports).
  12. Punishment evading will result in an extension of the original punishment and can result in a permanent ban.
  13. No team killing for any reason.
